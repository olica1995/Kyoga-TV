About Kyoga TV

Kyoga TV is a community-centered digital broadcast platform committed to empowering lives through transformative content. We are rooted in Northern Uganda with a mission to **inform, educate, entertain, and uplift our communities**, especially the youth and underserved populations.

Youth Empowerment

At the heart of Kyoga TV is the belief in the potential of young people. Through skill-based shows, talent showcases, mentorship sessions, and innovation-driven segments, we provide a platform for youth voices, creativity, and entrepreneurship.

Environmental Responsibility

We champion sustainable development and environmental conservation by airing content that raises awareness about climate change, proper waste management, tree planting, and the protection of natural ecosystems within our region and beyond.

Government Accountability

Kyoga TV plays a constructive role in strengthening democracy and good governance. We provide citizens with access to accurate, balanced, and timely information, while promoting transparency and civic responsibility in alignment with Uganda Communications Commission (UCC) regulations.

Entertainment & Culture

From music to drama, live events to talk shows — we celebrate Uganda’s diverse cultures while promoting content that is positive, inclusive, and respectful of national values. Our entertainment is both fun and thought-provoking, helping shape a vibrant and informed society.

Economic Growth

We support local businesses and entrepreneurs by giving them affordable access to digital advertising, interviews, and business exposure. Our programming inspires economic participation, innovation, and financial literacy at the grassroots level.

Compliance with UCC Guidelines

Kyoga TV upholds the highest standards of ethical broadcasting in line with the Uganda Communications Commission (UCC). We are committed to responsible journalism, accuracy, gender sensitivity, child protection, and content diversity.
